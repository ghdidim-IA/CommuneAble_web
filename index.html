About the Project

Technology that listens, responds & cares

CommuneAble is an innovative project designed to empower disabled and nonverbal individuals by providing them with tools to communicate effectively with others.


This project translates sign language to speech & writing It also converts speech to writing, and allows users to control mobile devices by voice!

How this project works ?

Here is the explanation of how CommuneAble works with an image below.

ESP32-S3

The ESP32-S3 contains an audio AI model that is trained for speech recognition, it sends the prediction value to the Arduino UNO R4 through Wi-Fi .

AI · Audio · Wi-Fi

Arduino UNO R4

The Arduino UNO R4 which is the access point of Wi-Fi, receive the audio prediction from ESP32-S3 & the sign prediction from ESP32-CAM then it turns on the writing machine & speaker

Wi-Fi · Access Piont

ESP32-CAM

The ESP32-CAM contains an AI model that is trained on sign language, it sends the prediction value to the Arduino UNO R4 through Wi-Fi

AI · Sign Language · Wi-Fi
